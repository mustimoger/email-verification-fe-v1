## Why Your Email Warm Up Services Aren't Working

You warmed up your domain for weeks. Engagement stays flat, bounce rates climb, and your ESP warns about sending quality.

The issue is often not the warmup tool. It is the list.

- **Invalid addresses** trigger hard bounces that tank sender reputation
- **Disposable emails** vanish after one send, so warmup effort is wasted
- **Catch-all domains** accept everything, then may bounce silently
- **Role-based addresses** like `info@` and `support@` rarely engage and can increase spam risk

Every bad email sent during warmup teaches mailbox providers to distrust you.

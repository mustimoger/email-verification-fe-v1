Efficiency is no longer about working faster — it’s about removing the work entirely. SaaS platforms do that by connecting data sources, triggering automation, and giving teams real-time visibility.

<Callout variant="success">
  Teams using automated workflows report fewer handoff delays and higher customer satisfaction.
</Callout>

## Four shifts we’re seeing in 2024

- **From manual to event-driven**: workflows trigger based on customer actions.
- **From static to live analytics**: dashboards update automatically.
- **From siloed to shared systems**: a single source of truth.
- **From reactive to proactive**: alerts surface issues early.
